Lab Teams Receive Honours at Engineering Research Project Competition

#awards #capstone #drone #flapping-propulsion #ray #robot

Two student teams from the lab received Honourable Mentions (佳作) at the College of Engineering Undergraduate Research Project Competition (專題研究成果競賽), with projects on an underwater bio-inspired stingray vehicle and a maple-seed-inspired unmanned aerial vehicle.

5 June 2026

Lab Teams Excel at NUST Scientific Paper Competition

#awards #capstone #drone #flapping-propulsion #ray #robot

Two undergraduate teams from the laboratory shared First Place in the Engineering Technology category at the National University System of Taiwan (NUST, 臺灣國立大學系統) Scientific Paper Competition (科技論文競賽), with projects inspired by stingrays and maple seeds. A postgraduate team also received an Honorable Mention Award (佳作) for its dragonfly-inspired drone research.
